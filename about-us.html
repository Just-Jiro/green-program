INFORMATION AND FAQ

What is G.R.E.E.N Program?

G.R.E.E.N Program (Great Recycling Ensures Efficient Nature) is a CAPSTONE Project that aims to solve the lingering problem of improper waste management in the households of Lipa City.

Not only that, it also encourages children to segregate at an early age through a set of activities.

Is it relevant?

Back in 2020, our team made an effort to research the households of Lipa City through surveys. According to the results presented, majority of the households are either unsure or don't have cleaning programs in their barangay/s; people not joining any cleaning programs as this may be a cause for the households not having any cleaning programs.

In response to this, our team has decided to create G.R.E.E.N Program prepared with multiple activities fun for both children and adults to address improper waste management.

What are the UNSDGs?

The program supports a wide variety of United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UNSDGs). 
Here's a list to show the relevant UNSDGs the program addresses.

- UNSDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production)

- UNSDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities)

- UNSDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation)

- UNSDG 3 (Good Health and Well-Being)

- UNSDG 13 (Climate Action)

- UNSDG 4 (Quality Education)

What can we expect?

In our program, you can expect a wide range of eco-friendly activities that are easy to do and follow.

Do we need to come in contact with people?

Since we're in the middle of a pandemic, our program's activities are entirely made online. There will be no contact with other people and our activities do not require people to go outside. 

In this way, you and your family/friends are able to enjoy the program in the comfort of your own home.

Where can we see the activities? How do we do them?

Our activities will be shown on our Facebook Page. You can also access our page by clicking the Facebook icon on the bottom of the website. If you're having trouble on the activities given, you can ask for help or support on our Facebook Page.

Activities!

Our program includes a lot of engaging activities ranging from webinars for people of all ages, to fun games for children. These set activities are meant to develop each person's critical thinking and overall, combat the harmful effects of improper waste management.

Examples of these activities include:
Art Week: An event or activity which children and possibly teenagers make an art out of recycled materials. 

Online Games: Our program will provide some online/browser interactive games for young children to play with. This will help them to become familiarized on how they will segregate their trash.


Chat Rooms: One of our activities will provide a chat room for children so that we can be more familiarized with them.


Checklists: Our program will give weekly checklists (3-4 activities per week) for children to practice recycling and proper segregation. Checklists will be seen on our Facebook page.
